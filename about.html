About the GCE Board

The Cameroon General Certificate of Education (GCE) Board is responsible for the organization, regulation, and administration of public examinations for O/L and A/L levels in Cameroon.

This portal has been created to make registration and result checking more efficient, secure, and accessible for students across Cameroon.
